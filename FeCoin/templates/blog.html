Purchase Guide

Step-by-Step Tutorial

1. Install TronLink

Download the TronLink wallet extension for Chrome or the mobile app.

2. Get USDT (TRC20)

Ensure you have USDT on the TRON network in your wallet.

3. Connect Wallet

Click the "Connect Wallet" button on the ZNST website.

4. Enter Amount

Type how much USDT you want to spend. The ZNST amount is calculated automatically.

5. Confirm

Click "Confirm Purchase" and approve the transaction in your wallet.
