1. Introduction

ZNST (Zinc Stable Token) represents a paradigm shift in the digital asset landscape. Unlike traditional stablecoins backed by fiat currencies subject to inflation, ZNST is a Real-World Asset (RWA) token directly pegged to the value of high-grade Zinc metal.

Zinc is the fourth most used metal in the world, essential for galvanizing steel, battery manufacturing, and modern infrastructure. By digitizing this critical industrial commodity, ZNST offers investors a hedging mechanism against inflation while providing industrial players with a seamless settlement layer.

2. The Problem

Inflationary Fiat Currencies

Traditional stablecoins (USDT, USDC) are pegged to the US Dollar. While they offer stability against crypto volatility, they are guaranteed to lose purchasing power over time due to central bank money printing. Holding fiat-backed stablecoins is holding a melting ice cube.

Barriers to Commodity Investment

Historically, investing in industrial metals like Zinc required futures contracts, expensive storage fees, or dealing with complex logistics on the London Metal Exchange (LME). Retail investors were effectively locked out.

3. The ZNST Solution

Value Proposition: ZNST democratizes access to the global Zinc market by tokenizing the metal on the TRON blockchain.

We bridge the gap between DeFi (Decentralized Finance) and Traditional Industry.

  • Inflation Hedge: Commodity prices historically rise during inflationary periods.
  • Industrial Utility: ZNST can be redeemed for physical Zinc warrants by verified industrial partners.
  • 24/7 Liquidity: Trade metal value instantly, without waiting for market opening hours.

4. Tokenomics & Peg Mechanics

ZNST maintains its peg through a rigorous reserve verification process and smart contract arbitrage.

The Peg Ratio

1 ZNST = Value of 1 KG of Special High Grade (SHG) Zinc (99.995% purity).

The price oracle feeds live data from the LME (London Metal Exchange) to our smart contracts, ensuring the minting and redemption prices always reflect the global spot price.

5. Technology Infrastructure

ZNST is built on the TRON Network (TRC-20 standard).

Why TRON?

  • High Throughput: Capable of 2,000 TPS, ensuring instant transactions.
  • Low Fees: Transaction costs are a fraction of a cent, making ZNST viable for micro-payments.
  • USDT Dominance: TRON hosts the largest supply of USDT, making swapping into ZNST frictionless.
